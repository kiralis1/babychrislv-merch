The Target & Walmart Shelf Plan

A real path onto a physical shelf — most kids' licensed brands start exactly here.

How brands actually get on the shelf

Target and Walmart don't take pitches straight from a YouTube channel — they buy from vendors with a track record. The real path looks like this:

  • Launch on Amazon, Etsy, and your own site first — build sales history and reviews
  • License the characters through a manufacturer who already sells into big-box retail
  • Get picked up by a sourcing agent who pitches retail buyers on your behalf
  • Start regional or online-only ("Target.com exclusive") before in-store placement
  • Have UPC codes, insurance, and production capacity ready before any buyer meeting

About the Family

The real family behind Baby Chris & LV.

The real explorers behind the show

Christian Lionel Estevan Westby and Tiffara Vasquez are the parents behind Baby Chris & LV — turning everyday moments with their kids into a channel built for curious toddlers and early learners.

The channel takes its name and inspiration straight from their own boys: Christian Westby Jr. ("Baby Chris") and Louis Vasquez ("LV"). Every episode — counting, the alphabet, jungle animals, ocean animals — comes from things they're already exploring and learning together as a family.

What started as home videos is growing into a full learning brand: new episodes, a website, and now a first look at merchandise that brings the characters off the screen.

Getting This Live

Practical next steps to take this from mockup to something sellable.

Step 1

Lock the domain

Top pick: babychrislv.com. Backups: babychrisandlv.com, chrisandlv.com. Check live availability on Namecheap or GoDaddy before building the real site.

Step 2

Print-on-demand first

Use Printful or Printify to launch apparel and posters with zero upfront inventory — connects directly to Etsy or a simple Shopify store.

Step 3

Books need a real printer

Board books and sound books aren't print-on-demand friendly — get quotes from a children's book printer once cover art is finalized.

Step 4

Protect the names

File a trademark for "Baby Chris & LV" and "Learning Is So Much Fun" before merch sales pick up — protects the brand once it's earning.
